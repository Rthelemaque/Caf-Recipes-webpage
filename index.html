Thelemaque Beanstalk Bistro

Mocha Caffeine Chronicles recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coffee beans
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions:

  1. Grind coffee beans.
  2. Boil water.
  3. Pour hot water over ground coffee in a cafeti√®re.
  4. Let steep for 4 minutes.
  5. Press down plunger slowly.
  6. Foam milk with a frother.
  7. Add sugar to taste.
  8. Pour coffee into a cup and add frothed milk.

Serving suggestion: Enjoy with a slice of chocolate cake.

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Note: For a stronger flavor, use more coffee beans.
